Welcome to the homepage of the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Lab at Tufts University!

The Tufts HCI Lab is directed by Professor Rob Jacob. Our research covers new interaction modes and techniques and user interface software; our current work focuses on implicit brain-computer interfaces. Lab members include Ph.D. and Master students, undergraduate researchers, post-docs and visiting scholars.

We are actively looking for motivated students to join our lab. If you are interested in human-computer interaction research, please email us your CV.
